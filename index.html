Services

A calm, one to one grooming experience tailored to your dog. Below are a few of the services I offer.

Full Groom

This service includes a luxurious bath, gentle brush out, and fluff dry.

I will style and trim to your preference, plus a nail trim, ear cleaning, and a finishing spritz of pet safe cologne so they leave looking and smelling their best.

Bath, Brush & Blow Dry

Ideal for dogs who just need a freshen up between grooms.

This service includes a gentle bath, a full brush through and a fluffy blow dry, Leaving your dog looking and smelling their best! Finished with a spritz of cologne.

Bath, Brush & Tidy

The perfect “freshen up” in between full grooms!

Your pup will enjoy a bubbly bath, brush, and blow dry, nails trimmed, paw pads neatened, visor/eye area trimmed, paws shaped, and a sanitary trim, so they stay clean, comfy, and cute until their next full spa day.

Puppy Groom

Once your pup is fully vaccinated, its the perfect time to start their grooming journey! My puppy grooms are designed to be gentle, positive and full of tail wags with plenty of cuddles, treats and encouragement!

This introduction includes a soothing bath, gentle brush, fluffy blowdry, nail trim, ear clean and a light tidy around the eyes/visor, paws and sanitary areas.

The pawfect way to set your puppy up for a lifetime of stress free grooming!

Ultrasonic Teeth Cleaning

Emmi-Pet uses patented ultrasonic technology to clean pet teeth without scrubbing or vibration, offering benefits like reduced plaque and tartar buildup, prevention of gingivitis and gum disease, elimination of bad breath, and a painless, silent, and stress-free experience for pets.

By promoting gum circulation, it also helps heal wounds and reduces the need for anesthetic-based dental cleanings, making it a safe, non-invasive option for daily oral hygiene

The Pampered Pup Spa Package

Treat your pup to the ultimate pamper session!

This service includes:

- A refreshing melon & cucumber foaming facial.

- A nourishing avocado infused paw & nose balm.

- A standard teeth clean to keep those smiles fresh!

Perfect for adding a touch of indulgence to any groom, leaving your pup looking, feeling and smelling amazing!

About

Hi! I’m Lucy, a professional dog groomer with over 7 years of experience working in multiple salons as well as running my own services. My passion for grooming began at 14 years old, when I watched my own Bichon Frisé being transformed on the grooming table - I knew right then what I wanted to do.

I started out as a bather, learning the essentials of washing, drying and brushing, before studying dog grooming at Merrist Wood College in Guildford to refine my skills. After college, I worked in professional salons for several years, gaining invaluable hands-on experience with a wide variety of breeds and styles. Since then, I’ve had the privilege of grooming hundreds, maybe even thousands, of dogs of every size, breed and personality.

Whether it’s a tiny toy breed or a giant fluffball, a confident pup or a nervous newcomer, I believe every dog deserves a positive grooming experience. My goal is to create a calm, safe, and fun environment where every dog leaves looking and feeling their very best.
